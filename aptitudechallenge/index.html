Get excited for our next initiative #10DaysofAptituteChallenge. We are taking an initiative of #learnfromhome. where you can prepare yourself for the aptitude, interview and make the best of your vacation.
Date: 16thJune to 25thJune 2020.

About the Challenge:
Challenge is very easy, We will give 2 probs on Quantitative, Logical and Verbal each. Read more about the daily task here.
Each day, morning at 8am, we'll release the 6 problems (someday 5). Participants have to submit the solution on the same day before 11:59 pm.
Top Performer will receive the $200 Google Cloud Platform credits, which can be use for Development work.

How to submit the solution?
Create Google Drive Directory / Github Repo and share the link. Naming Conversion: <PID>_10DaysofAptitudeChallenge and subfolder of each day. Check out the example here
We expect your original work. If you are cheating in this challenge, you are also cheating on your life as well.
